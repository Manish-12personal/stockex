Calculation

Gross Win   = 5 × 1.95 = 9.75 Tickets  = ₹2,925.00
Profit      = 9.75 - 5 = 4.75 Tickets  = ₹1,425.00
Brokerage   = 5% of ₹1,425             = ₹71.25
Net Profit  = ₹1,425 - ₹71.25          = ₹1,353.75

Wallet Credit = ₹1,500 (bet refund) + ₹1,353.75 (profit) = ₹2,853.75

Key Points

  • 5 Tickets × ₹300 = ₹1,500 lagaya DOWN pe
  • Jeeta → ₹2,853.75 wallet mein automatically credit (₹1,353.75 net profit)
  • Haara → ₹1,500 gaya, kuch nahi milega
  • UP ya DOWN — dono pe jeet sakte ho, market direction decide karega
  • Brokerage sirf profit pe lagta hai (5%), full amount pe nahi
  • Winnings automatically Games Wallet mein aa jaati hai
